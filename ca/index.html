Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Clara and how it works for your business.

How does Clara work?

When someone calls your business, Clara answers on your behalf — in a friendly, natural voice that sounds like you. Clara chats with the caller, takes down their details, and sends you a short, clear summary of what they needed.

Do I need a new phone number?

No! Clara works with your existing phone number. Just connect your current business line and Clara will start handling calls when you can't pick up.

Can Clara sound like my business?

Absolutely. You can customize Clara's voice and tone to match your brand — whether that's friendly and casual, professional and confident, or calm and reassuring.

What happens if someone calls after hours?

Clara can answer calls 24/7, taking messages and collecting details even when you're closed. You can set specific business hours, and Clara will let callers know when you'll be back.

Is Clara an AI or a real person?

Clara is an AI-powered receptionist that uses natural language to have real conversations with your callers. Clara sounds human, responds intelligently, and gets better with every call.

How long does setup take?

Setup takes less than 5 minutes. Just connect your phone number, answer a few questions about your business, choose Clara's voice, and you're ready to go.
