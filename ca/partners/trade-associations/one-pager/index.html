Product overview

What members see after Clara answers

Clara is an AI voice receptionist for small service businesses. Members can forward missed calls from their existing number or use Clara as a second business number. Clara answers professionally, collects the details the business cares about, then turns each call into a short, prioritised work item.

Prioritised inbox

Calls land in a simple inbox with caller, time, and a plain-English summary, so owners can quickly see what needs attention first.

Call summary card

Each call becomes a clear card with the job type, location, key details, urgency, and an obvious call-back action.

Transcript and recording

Members can review the transcript or listen back when they need the exact details, without relying on voicemail notes.
