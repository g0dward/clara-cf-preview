Clara for Removals

When someone needs a move, they don't leave voicemails

Explore how Clara can help your removals business succeed

Does this sound familiar?

When you're on a job, you can't always answer the phone. Most removal calls are time-sensitive. A house move next weekend, a date change, a last-minute office relocation. When someone calls and nobody picks up, they move on to the next company. Clara makes sure every call is answered, every detail is captured, and every lead reaches you — even when you're mid-move.

Removals jobs start with a quote, and the quote starts with a phone call. A household planning a move calls three or four companies, talks to whoever picks up, and usually goes with the first one that felt professional and gave them a clear sense of what to expect. The company that calls back two days later — after the caller has already booked someone else — doesn't get the job.

For small removals companies, the window to win new work is measured in hours, not days. Customers are making a time-sensitive decision about something important and stressful. They want to speak to someone who sounds like they know what they're doing and can give them a date. If nobody answers, they move to the next number.

The jobs are high-value — a local house move typically runs to several hundred or a few thousand — which means the revenue impact of even a handful of missed calls per week is significant. And the nature of removals work makes answering nearly impossible during the day: the crew is loading a van, carrying furniture, or in transit. The phone sits in a pocket or in the cab, and the enquiry that came in at 10am won't be seen until 4pm. By then, the caller has sorted it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can Clara handle quote requests?

Clara captures everything needed for a quote — property size, locations, dates, and any large items — and sends it all to you. You follow up with the price, fully prepared.

Will Clara know what questions to ask about removals?

Clara is configured to ask the right questions for your trade: where they're moving, property size, dates, and access details. You can customise these at any time.

What if a customer calls to change their moving date?

Clara takes a message with the details and lets you know so you can update the schedule when you're free. Clara will let the caller know you'll be in touch shortly.

Does Clara work after hours?

Absolutely. Clara answers calls 24/7. For after-hours calls, Clara can let callers know your business hours and still capture their details so nothing is lost.

Will callers know they're speaking to an AI?

Clara sounds natural and conversational — most callers won't realise. You can choose whether Clara introduces herself as an assistant or simply answers on your behalf. It's up to you.

Can Clara give out my pricing?

You can set up Clara to share your standard rates, or simply let callers know you'll provide a quote once you've reviewed the job details. You're in control of what Clara says.

Do I need a new phone number?

No. Clara works with your existing business number. Just set up call forwarding and Clara starts answering when you can't pick up.

What if someone calls about something Clara can't handle?

Clara will take a message and flag it for you. Clara won't try to bluff — if something's outside what it can handle, Clara takes a message and makes sure you get the details so you can call back.
