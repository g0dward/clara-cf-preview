Questions worth asking

Everything a busy business owner needs to know before getting started.

Do I need to change my phone number?

No. Clara works with your existing business number. There's nothing to port or change — just connect your number in the app and Clara starts answering when you can't.

How much does it cost?

Clara starts at £49.99/month. There are no per-minute charges or hidden fees — just one flat monthly price. You can try it free before committing.

Will my customers know they're talking to an AI?

Clara sounds natural and conversational, not robotic. Most callers don't realise. You can also customise the name and voice so it feels like your own receptionist.

What if I'm a one-person business?

That's exactly who Clara is built for. When you're on a job and can't answer, Clara picks up, takes the details, and sends you a summary — so you never lose a lead while you're working.

What kind of businesses use Clara?

Plumbers, electricians, builders, landscapers, salon owners, consultants — any small or growing business that gets calls and can't always answer them. If missed calls are costing you work, Clara fixes that.

How long does setup take?

Under 5 minutes. Download the app, connect your number, answer a few questions about your business, and you're live. No technical knowledge required.

What happens outside business hours?

Clara can answer calls around the clock. Set your business hours and Clara will let callers know when you're back — or take a message any time of day or night.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Clara is built with privacy and security at its core. Your call data is encrypted and never sold or shared with third parties.
